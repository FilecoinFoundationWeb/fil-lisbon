FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions
So many events happening in Lisbon, what does a FIL Lisbon ticket include?

FIL Lisbon includes the Filecoin Interplanetary Party (Oct. 30) and FIL Lisbon Main Stage Days (Oct. 31 - Nov. 1). Lookout for the registration links for the side events hosted at the many different venues around Lisbon, just make sure to apply the “Side Events” filter in the schedule. Our online schedule is constantly updated, so check daily!

Also, there are loads of other events planned as part of Lisbon Blockchain Month. Learn more at their website.

I’m hosting a community side event, how can I submit my event to appear on your website?

It’s amazing that you are hosting a side event! Please fill out this form, and once approved, it will appear on our website schedule with the “Side Events” filter. Please allow us a couple of days for review.

What’s the “Hacker Base”?

The Hacker Base, hosted by Filecoin Foundation x Fission Codes x Lightshift, is a co-working space when it’s not in use for private events. In case you would like to use this co-working space for work or small private meetings, please register here.

Check out what’s going on at the Hacker Base here! Make sure the “Hacker Base” filter is on and check it daily, as it is constantly updated.

Where do I stay in Lisbon?

Lisbon is a very accessible city, with an abundance of hotels, guest houses and B&Bs! Check out The Agora House at the LX Hostel for a community living experience!

How do I get around Lisbon?

LX Factory is easily reachable by tram, taxi, uber or on foot if you’re an explorer! We also have available a 20% discount on BIRD Scooters from Oct. 31 - Nov. 4, download the app and apply the code “FILXBIRD” on your ride!

What’s the COVID Policy for this event?

Your safety is our priority at FIL Lisbon! Please review our recommendations here for COVID-19. Note that there is inherent risk of exposure to COVID-19 for in-person attendees.  If you are feeling sick or have any COVID-19 symptoms, please skip in-person meetings!
